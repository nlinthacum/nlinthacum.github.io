My collection of exciting projects

Dorm room thermostat

I struggled keeping my dorm room at a comfortable temperature. Thus, I made a device that controls my fan according to the set temperature and the current temperature in my room. After completing an initial prototype, as shown in the picture, I designed a printed circuit board to make the thermostat a more permanent solution. 

View Project

Wine Fridge Temp Text Alerts

My parent's wine fridge would not stay at the desired temperature, resulting in frozen and spoiled bottles of wine. Because of this issue, I made a device that would send a text message alert when the temperature inside the wine fridge exceeded a range. 

Solar Panel Cleaning Robot

Designed robot that moves around and senses when it is about to go off an edge. Ongoing/Future goal of moving autonomously with high precision to be able to run on actual home solar panels.
