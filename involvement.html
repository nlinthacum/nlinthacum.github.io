Embedded Team Lead 

Gonzaga RoboSub

(Embedded Systems and Machine Learning Teams)

GU RoboSub is a student led club that designs and builds an autonomous submarine to compete in the RoboNation competition. The Embedded Systems team is responsible for low-level control of the sub (motors, interfacing, sensors, etc). The Machine Learning team is currently working on a proof of concept process. 

Team Member 

New Venture Lab

New Venture Lab is Gonzaga's student run consulting organization that works with entrepreneurs to develop new products and business ventures.  My most recent work was with Sightlife, developing a target product profile for a post-cornea surgery implant immunosuppressant. Most of my work involved supply-side research on existing technologies and developments.

Paid Researcher

Math + Computer Science Dept.

I am doing research regarding the use of stacks, queues, deques, and torus deques to process edges of various graphs. My work includes finding patterns among families of graphs, discovering how certain graphs can be graphed using different data structures, and determining planarity of graphs on various orientable and non-orientable surfaces.
